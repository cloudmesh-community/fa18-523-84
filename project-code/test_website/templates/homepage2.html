Info Search for Higher Education Institutions in Indiana


Welcome to the Info search page for Colleges and Universities located in Indiana. There are many great institutions throughout the state and the aim of this website is to help you navigate your options. First please take a moment to tell us a bit about yourself. This will help to see what schools may be a good fit based on SAT scores and admission rates. The "Key Metrics by Institution" page will allow you to either search for a specific school, school system or the entire list of schools. The "Compare Schools to the State Average" page is a great place to see how schools that you are interested in compare to the state average. Lastly, the "What School is Right for Me?" page will show you how your scores match up against the average for each school.
